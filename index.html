The story of La Boomerie

1. La Boomerie creation

La Boomerie is the result of a common passion based on underground electronic music (House music more precisely), and a real desire to organize music events principally based between Paris and Lille. This resulted in our volunteers to become actors of this scene and not only spectators. Our vision is to promote artists coming from all around Europe and make it possible for them to share their music in front of a large and passionate public
